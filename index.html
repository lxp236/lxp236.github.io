Welcome to the Nanoscale Electro-Optics Lab (NEOL)! Our team focuses on exploring the intricate world of nanoscale photonic physics and its exciting applications by leveraging the platform of integrate photonics with high-speed electronics. Our interests span both theory and experiment, with a keen focus on cutting-edge research areas such as photonic neuromorphic computing, quantum optics, nonlinear optics, microwave photonics, Photonic neuromorphic computing, and optical frequency combs, etc.
